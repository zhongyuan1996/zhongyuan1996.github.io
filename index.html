About Me

I am a 2nd-year PhD student in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Pennsylvania State University. I am co-advised by Prof. Suhang Wang and Prof. Xiang Zhang. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology at Chongqing University in 2023, where I was fortunately supervised by Prof. Lei Feng and Prof. Jiaxing Shang.

Research Interests

My recent research interests focus on multimodal learning, generative models (e.g., large language models, large vision-language models, and diffusion models), and trustworthy artificial intelligence—including safety, robustness, and privacy—with a particular emphasis on their intersections.
