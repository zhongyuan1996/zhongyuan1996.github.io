About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in the College of Information Sciences and Technology (IST) at Penn State University. I am advised by Prof. Fenglong Ma. Previously, I obtained my Master's degree in Mathematical Finance at Boston University and my Bachelor's degree in Statistics at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign..

Research Interests

My recent research interests lie in Diffusion Models, Multimodal Generation, Generative Retrieval, Recommendation Models, Health Informatics, and LLMs.
